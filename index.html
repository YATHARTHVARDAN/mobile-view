Why Follege?

"We all know that college is the time for networking and exploration , but despute that the harsh reality is like we don't even know about our own classmates, let alone the other college students.

Also ,in a country like INDIA where the education infrastructure is not strong , there is a huge gap between students and administration.

And these big social platforms like FB-Instagram,they are nothing but a sea of information , due to which 60-70% of important information is often missed out!
